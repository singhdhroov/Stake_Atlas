Doug's Take

We love the Porsche 944 here on Cars & Bids, thanks to its fantastic handling, nice balance, and handsome 1980s styling. This particular example is a desirable 944 S, boasting a more powerful 2.5-liter 4-cylinder in combination with a 5-speed manual transmission. This 944 also benefits from a freshly rebuilt engine, which should provide some major peace of mind to the next owner – and it's finished in the desirable Guards Red, boasting few modifications and a clean, accident-free Carfax report, which always adds to the appeal.

Highlights

THIS... is a 1987 Porsche 944 S Coupe, finished in Guards Red with a black leather interior.

  • This Porsche 944 S is equipped with the desirable 5-speed manual transmission.
  • The attached Carfax history report has a few gaps, but it shows no accidents for the given time period. It also shows that this coupe has been registered in California and Nevada since at least 1994.
  • Factory equipment on this 944 S includes a sunroof, leather upholstery, air conditioning, cruise control, a digital clock, and more as listed below.
  • Modifications reported by the seller include Koni rear shocks, 16-inch "Phone Dial" style wheels, tinted windows, and a Sony stereo.
  • Service documentation in the photo gallery indicates that this 944 had an engine-out rebuild performed in November 2022.
  • Porsche introduced the 944 in 1983 as an evolution of the 924, and positioned it as its entry-level model for most of the 1980s. It gradually expanded the range with a mid-level 944 S and a flagship Turbo model. Production ended after the 1991 model year, and the 944 was replaced by the 968 in 1992.
  • 1987 was the first model year for the Porsche 944 S. The S, which stood for "Super," was a high-performance variant of the front-engine, rear-wheel-drive 944. It included an upgraded engine with dual-overhead cams, 16 valves, and a self-adjusting timing belt tensioner. Other improvements over the standard 944 include progressive springs, thicker sway bars, and a revised transmission.
  • Power comes from an "M44/40" 2.5-liter inline-4 engine, rated at 190 horsepower and 170 lb-ft of torque. Output is sent to the rear wheels via a 5-speed manual transmission.
